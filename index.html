About the Cornerstone Campaign

Why the Cornerstone Campaign?

For years, Gosen Haitian SDA Church has faithfully served the Haitian and Creole community in Jacksonville, Florida. Through seasons of renting, sharing spaces, and adapting to limited resources, our congregation has never stopped worshipping, growing, and serving. Every Sabbath, families gather to praise God, study His Word, and lift one another up. But we have always done so in borrowed spaces, with no permanent place to call our own.

The name Cornerstone comes from Psalm 118:22, which says, “The stone the builders rejected has become the cornerstone.” This verse speaks to the heart of who we are. Like that rejected stone, our community has been overlooked, underestimated, and under-resourced. But God has a plan. He is taking what the world dismissed and making it the foundation for something lasting, something powerful, something beautiful.

God has opened a door. We have identified a beautiful property at 8819 103rd St, Jacksonville, FL 32210 that is perfect for our permanent church home. This single-story brick building offers the space, the layout, and the character we need to serve our congregation and community for generations to come. The time to act is now, and we are stepping forward in faith.

What Your Gift Makes Possible

⛪

A Sanctuary for Worship

A permanent place where families gather every Sabbath to worship, pray, sing, and grow in faith together. No more renting. No more uncertainty. A home that is truly ours, where we can worship freely and fully.

📚

Youth Classrooms

Dedicated spaces for Bible study, tutoring, mentoring, and nurturing the next generation of leaders. Our children and teens deserve a place where they can learn, ask questions, and discover their God-given purpose.

🤝

Fellowship Hall

A community gathering space for outreach events, health seminars, cooking classes, holiday celebrations, and service programs that bless our neighbors and strengthen our bonds as a church family.

🏠

A Safe Gathering Space

A welcoming home where families can connect, find support, build lasting friendships, and grow together in a place that reflects the love of God. A place where everyone who walks through the door feels they belong.

The total campaign goal is $800,000.00. Why Monthly Giving Changes Everything

Research shows that monthly donors give 42% more annually than one-time donors. A gift of $50 each month becomes $600 in a year. A gift of $100 each month becomes $1,200. Over the life of our campaign, monthly givers will be the backbone of our success.

Monthly giving also helps our church plan with confidence. When we know consistent support is coming in, we can negotiate with sellers, schedule renovations, and prepare for move-in with certainty. Your recurring gift is not just generous. It is strategic.

Setting up monthly giving takes less than two minutes through Adventist Giving or our online form. You can adjust or cancel at any time. There is no long-term obligation, just a powerful way to make a lasting difference.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.” 2 Corinthians 9:7. We do not ask you to give out of obligation. We ask you to give out of love, out of faith, and out of a shared vision for what God is doing in Jacksonville. Whatever you give, give with joy. God will honor it.

How We Track and Share Progress

We believe in complete transparency. Every dollar donated to the Cornerstone Campaign is tracked, recorded, and reported to our congregation and donors. The thermometer above is updated regularly to reflect total contributions received from all giving channels: Adventist Giving, Zelle, mail, in-person, and online donations.

Our church treasurer maintains detailed records of all gifts, and our campaign committee reviews financial reports monthly. Quarterly updates are sent to all donors via mail and email, showing exactly how much has been raised, how many donors have contributed, and what milestones have been reached.

We do this because your trust matters more than anything. When you give to the Cornerstone Campaign, you deserve to know exactly where your money is going and how close we are to our goal. Transparency is not just a policy for us. It is a promise.
